System of equations can be modelled as an operation of vectors and matrices

$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
3x + 2y &=& 8 \\
7x + y &=& 15 \\
\end{eqnarray}$$



Can be written as

$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
\begin{bmatrix} 3 & 2 \\ 7 & 1 \end{bmatrix} *
\begin{bmatrix} x \\ y  \end{bmatrix} = 
\begin{bmatrix} 8 \\ 15  \end{bmatrix}
\end{eqnarray}$$
