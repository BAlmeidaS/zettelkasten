*Union* in fuzzy sets are the **maximum** of both [[Membership Function|membership functions]]:

$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
\mu_{A\bigcup B} &=& max[\mu_A(x), \mu_B(x)] \\
&=& \mu_A(x) \cup \mu_B(x) 

\end{eqnarray}$$
