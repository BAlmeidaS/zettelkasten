$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
tanh(x) = \dfrac{e^z-e^{-z}}{e^z+e^{-z}}
\end{eqnarray}$$

\dfrac{d(tanh())}{dz}

\dfrac{d\left(\dfrac{e^z-e^{-z}}{e^z+e^{-z}}\right)}{dz} 

\end{eqnarray}$$
