[[Limit]] of a [[function]] calculated by approaching "by the left" or *left limit* or *from below*. It **has the signal $\displaystyle \large -$**
$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
\lim_{x\to a^-}f(x) = l
\end{eqnarray}$$
