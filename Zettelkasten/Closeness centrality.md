A [[Vertices|vertice]] is important *if it has small shortest path lengths to all other nodes*

$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
c_v = \dfrac{1}{\sum_{u\ne v}\text{shortest path length between u and v}}
\end{eqnarray}$$
