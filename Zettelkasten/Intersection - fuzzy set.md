*Intersection* in fuzzy sets are the **minimum** of both [[Membership Function|membership functions]]:

$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
\mu_{A\bigcap B} &=& min[\mu_A(x), \mu_B(x)] \\
&=& \mu_A(x) \cap \mu_B(x) 

\end{eqnarray}$$
