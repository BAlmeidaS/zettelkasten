[[Limit]] of a [[function]] calculated by approaching "by the right" or *right limit* or *from above*. It **has the signal $\displaystyle \large +$**
$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
\lim_{x\to a^+}f(x) = l
\end{eqnarray}$$
