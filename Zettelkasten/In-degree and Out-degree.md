In [[Directed Graphs]] the [[Node Degree]] is measured of amount that is going in and out from the node. Represented as:
$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
k_{node}^{in} \text{ and } k_{node}^{out}
\end{eqnarray}$$
