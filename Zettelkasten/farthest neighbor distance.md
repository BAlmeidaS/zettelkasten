A type of [[Dissimilarity metrics for clustering]], given to clusters $\displaystyle \large C_1$ and $\displaystyle \large C_2$:
$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
D_{fn}(C_1, C_2) &=& \max_{i \in C_1,\ j\in C_2}d(y_i, z_j)
\end{eqnarray}$$
