A [[function]] $\displaystyle \large f$ is *continuous* at $\displaystyle \large a$ if 
$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
\lim_{x \to a} f(x) = f(a)  
\end{eqnarray}$$

Remember that, to such [[Limit]] exist, [[left limit]] and [[right limit]] must be the same
