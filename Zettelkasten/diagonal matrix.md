A [[matrix]] is a *diagonal matrix* if the only elements that are different than 0 are in their diagonal:
$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
\begin{bmatrix}  
a_1 & 0 & \dots & 0 \\ 
0 & a_2 & \dots & 0 \\ 
\vdots & \vdots& \ddots &\vdots\\
0 & 0 & \dots & a_n \\ 
\end{bmatrix}
\end{eqnarray}$$
