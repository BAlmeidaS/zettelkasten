A good representation for [[Graphs|Graph]]. Space efficient for **Large and Sparse** networks

Basically a *dictionary* with:
- keys as *source nodes*
- values as a *list of neighbour nodes*
$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
a&:& \\
b&:& c, d \\
c&:& b, d \\
d&:& b, c, d \\
\vdots
\end{eqnarray}$$
