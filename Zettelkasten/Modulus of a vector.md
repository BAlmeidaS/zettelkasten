Modulus of a [[vector]] $\displaystyle \large v$ is defined as:
$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
v &=& \begin{bmatrix} a \\ b \end{bmatrix} \\
|v| &=& \sqrt{a^2+b^2}

\end{eqnarray}$$
