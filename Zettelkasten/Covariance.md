The way that two [[Random Variable]] ([[Continuous variable]]) vary together, the way they *co-vary*
$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
cov(x, y) &=& E[(x-\bar{x})(y-\bar{y})]
\end{eqnarray}$$

Being $\displaystyle \large E[z]$ the [[Expected Value]]
