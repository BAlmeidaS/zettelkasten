An **orthogonal** [[matrix]] is a *square matrix* whose columns and rows are [[orthonormal vectors]].

If $\displaystyle \large A$ is an *orthogonal matrix*, so:
$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
A^T &=& A^{-1} \\
&\text{and}& \\
det(A) &=& 1\text{ or } \textminus1\\ 

\end{eqnarray}$$
