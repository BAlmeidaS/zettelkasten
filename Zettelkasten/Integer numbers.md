*Integer numbers* or *Integers* are defined as $\displaystyle \large \mathbb{Z}$

It is the set of all integer numbers, *negative, zero, and positive*:

$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
\mathbb{Z} &=& \{\dots, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, \dots\}
\end{eqnarray}$$
