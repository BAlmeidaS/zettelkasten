A *Graph $\displaystyle \large G$* is defined by the *nodes $\displaystyle \large N$* and the *edges $\displaystyle \large E$*, such as that it can be defined as:
$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
G(N, E)
\end{eqnarray}$$
