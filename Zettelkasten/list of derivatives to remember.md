list of useful [[derivative]] to remember

$$\displaystyle \Huge \begin{eqnarray} 
f(x) &=& e^x&, &\ f^\prime(x)=e^x \\\\
f(x) &=& ln(x)&, &\ f^\prime(x)=\dfrac{1}{x} \\\\
f(x) &=& sin(x)&, &\ f^\prime(x)=cos(x) \\\\
f(x) &=& cos(x)&, &\ f^\prime(x)=-sin(x) \\\\
\end{eqnarray}$$
